Privacy Policy – Newsletter

This privacy policy explains how we process your personal data in connection with the sending of our newsletter and the operation of our website.

  1. Controller
    Jan Düver
    Address TBA
    info@WeeklyGameVault.com
  2. Collection and Processing of Personal Data
    2.1. Newsletter Subscription
    When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is used exclusively for sending the newsletter.
  3. Legal Basis for Data Processing
    3.1. Newsletter Subscription
    The legal basis for processing your email address is your voluntarily given consent in accordance with Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR.
  4. Newsletter Registration and Dispatch
    We use a double opt-in procedure to register for our newsletter. This means that after you register, you will receive an email in which you must confirm your registration. Only after this confirmation will your email address be added to the distribution list.
  5. Contents of the Newsletter
    In our newsletter, we regularly inform you about available free games from platforms like Prime Gaming and Epic Games.
  6. Storage Duration
    Your data will be stored for as long as you have subscribed to the newsletter. After you unsubscribe, your email address will be removed from the distribution list.
  7. Data Transfer
    Your data will not be passed on to third parties, except as necessary for the operation of our services (e.g., using Google Analytics for website analytics).
  8. Your Rights
    You have the right to:
    • Access to your stored data
    • Rectification of incorrect data
    • Deletion of your data
    • Restriction of data processing
    • Data portability
    • Objection to data processing
  9. Withdrawal of Consent and Unsubscribe
    You can revoke your consent to the storage of your data and its use for the purpose of sending the newsletter at any time by clicking on the unsubscribe link at the end of each newsletter or by sending us a message to info@WeeklyGameVault.com.
  10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
    We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Specifically:
    • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the basic functioning of our website and cannot be disabled. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our website.
    • Google Analytics Cookies: We use Google Analytics to understand how our website is used and to improve your experience. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit our site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our site.
  11. Legal Basis for Cookies and Google Analytics
    The legal basis for using cookies and Google Analytics is your consent, which you provide by agreeing to our cookie policy via the Cookie-Script consent banner on our website.
  12. Managing Your Consent
    Upon your first visit, a consent banner will appear, allowing you to accept all cookies or manage your preferences. You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking on the Cookie-Script icon in the footer of our website.
  13. Changes to This Privacy Policy
    We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time. The current version can be found on our website.
