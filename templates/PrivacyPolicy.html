Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This privacy policy explains how we process your personal data in connection with the sending of our newsletter, the operation of our website, and our Telegram bot service.

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is used exclusively for sending the newsletter.

Telegram Bot Service

When you interact with our Telegram bot, we collect and process:

  • Your Telegram chat ID

Telegram Bot Service

The processing of your Telegram data is based on your explicit consent, which you provide by accepting this privacy policy when using the /subscribe command.

Newsletter Registration and Dispatch

We use a double opt-in procedure to register for our newsletter. This means that after you register, you will receive an email in which you must confirm your registration.

Contents of the Newsletter

In our newsletter, we regularly inform you about available free games from platforms like Prime Gaming and Epic Games.

Storage Duration

Your data will be stored for as long as you have subscribed to the newsletter. After you unsubscribe, your email address will be removed from the distribution list.

Telegram Bot Data

  • Unsubscribed users: Data is retained with inactive status until deletion is requested

Data Transfer

Your data will not be passed on to third parties, except as necessary for the operation of our services (e.g., using Google Analytics for website analytics).

For Telegram bot users, your data is stored securely and is only accessed to provide the notification service.
